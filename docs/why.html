Why Vowrite

Why not use the fastest way to input?

Every day you have countless ideas to convey: emails, messages, notes, documents. But you choose the slowest way — typing one character at a time.

All you really need is to get your ideas out.


Your input method should keep up with your brain, not slow it down. When you already have a complete thought, why spend a minute typing it out?


Your mouth is much faster than your fingers. From thought to speech, there's almost no delay. Speaking is the most natural, most efficient form of human output — but past voice input was too rough: inaccurate recognition, filler words everywhere, messy formatting.

It's not that voice input doesn't work — the tools weren't good enough.

Now, the tools have caught up.

You say 'um so basically let's have a meeting tomorrow at 3pm to discuss' and the screen shows:

Meeting tomorrow at 3pm.

It doesn't record what you said — it captures what you meant. Clean, precise, done in one step.

Our Vision

We believe input should be invisible. You shouldn't think about how to input — you should just think, speak, and your ideas arrive exactly as you mean them. Vowrite is the bridge between your mind and the screen.

Our Ambition

Today, Vowrite is a smart voice keyboard. Tomorrow, it's your input layer — adapting your words to any context, connecting to any workflow, speaking your language in every sense. Voice is just the beginning.

Our Goal

Make input effortless for everyone. No learning curve. No configuration headache. Just speak, and your ideas arrive — clean, precise, and unmistakably yours.

The best input is the most natural one.

Say it once. Mean it perfectly.
